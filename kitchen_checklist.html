Cleaning the Kitchen

  1. Clear counters and table
    1. Food
    2. Trash
    3. Gather dishes
      • Coffeepot, if dirty
    4. Mail in mail container
    5. Misc objects returned to places
    6. dirty place-mats/napkins
  2. Dishes
    1. Empty dish rack
      • Sharpen any knives
    2. Empty dishwasher
      • Sharpen any knives
    3. Load dishwasher
    4. Run dishwasher
      1. Change sign to say ‘clean’
    5. Hand wash special dishes and load onto drying rack
    6. Wash sink
  3. Wash counter, stove-top, and table-top
    1. Wipe counter for crumbs
    2. Wipe stove-top for crumbs
    3. Spray counter with granite spray
    4. Spray stove-top with glass spray
    5. Spray table with wood spray
    6. Scrub with rags
  4. Rags go to laundry room
  5. Take out rugs
  6. Sweep
  7. Mop (if necessary)
  8. Replace rugs
  9. Take out trash and replace trash bag
    1. Take big trashcan out if it is Tuesday night
    2. Take out recycling, if full
  11. Any other miscellaneous tasks
